Laboratory

This space does not exist to show what I already know.
It exists to record what I am still learning.

Code is just a tool, not the goal.
Learning by making mistakes and starting over,
guided by curiosity.

This is a laboratory for experiments:
small scripts, unfinished ideas,
human and honest attempts.

Some things will turn out nice and polished.
Others won’t.
And that’s okay — truly.

Maybe one day this becomes a portfolio.
Maybe it doesn’t.

One thing is certain: this is the path of someone, close to 40,
who keeps learning how to code —
not automatically, not mechanically,
but by thinking, creating, and reflecting on life.
