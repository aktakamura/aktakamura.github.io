01/14/2025 — freedom

Organizing your thoughts takes time.

And it’s exhausting.

But trying to remember everything
using only your head
is much worse.

No one will tell you what you need to remember.
At work, maybe a micromanaging boss will.
But in your life, no one will.

One thought at a time.
One less microtask occupying your mind.

And when you least expect it,
a flow of deliveries,
a whirlwind of actions.

You’ll realize your life was already moving.
It was your mind that was stuck.

And that peace
is true freedom.

2025-12-16

I had a meeting scheduled.

One of those where I would explain, for the umpteenth time, a dashboard that was supposedly finished. With all dataflows validated.

Suddenly, it was canceled.

And the relief came like lightning.

The day felt brighter. A little cooler. Lighter.

Energy. Silence. Peace.

It made me think that maybe the problem is not the meeting. Not the people. Not the job.

Maybe it is the role I play in it. Maybe it is being “the BI guy”.

Translating the obvious. Sustaining a ritual that no longer moves me. That no longer gives me meaning.

I do not have answers today.

Only the clear feeling that something emptied. And that says more about me than about the job.

Still under construction. Like me.
